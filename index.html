Self-Service Kiosk

A digital touch screen where customers can browse the menu, make selections and customize their order. When they're ready, they can place the order and make payment on their own. Simple - focusing on customer-oriented functions and features, but remain uncomplicated, these what makes iHartKiosk a faster and smoother ordering experience.

Mobile App

A simple, fast, and easy to use ordering system app that allows customers to conveniently place orders from their smartphone in their own comfort. The app will be publish in Apple Store and Google play.

Web Order System

Give your customers the freedom to place orders from their computer, tablet or smartphone. As long they have an internet, they can visit the website, browse the menu, place the order and make a payment. Safe, secure, easy and mobile friendly. Simplicity as key – keeping it simple as an art.

Kitchen Display

Our system allows a paperless, streamlined ordering process - from customers' fingertips directly to the kitchen. That’s why no matter where the orders come from, whether Self-Service Kiosk, Mobile App or Web Order, they will appear on a digital screen that you can set up in the kitchen. Say hello to simplicity and efficiency!
